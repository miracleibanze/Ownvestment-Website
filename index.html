Step 01.

Buy Ownvest in singapore Dollars.

We divide properties into unique assets called 'Ownvest'. Each Ownvest represents a fraction of the current value of the property.

Ownvest are purchased good old Singapore Dollars so there's no need for expensive exchanges.

Step 02.

Earn Rental Income.

Your Ownvests generate the quoted rental yield per month in proportion to how many Ownvest of the property you own.

Watch your income grow in real time and withdraw earned funds at any time.

Step 03.

Grow Capital Gains.

The value of your Ownvest are adjusted in-line with annual evaluations.

This means your asset's potential for capital growth is directly inline with the propery's market value.

Step 04.

Sell Your Ownvestment

Cash out at any moment by selling your Ownvest at the click of a button.

Capture capital gains with confidence on the only liquidity on focused Property Platform.
